I'm Sohyeon, a PhD student in the Media, Technology, and Society program @ Northwestern, advised by Aaron Shaw and part of the Community Data Science Collective :) My studies, centered around online governance, are generously supported by the NSF GRFP (DGE-1842165).

about

+ a spiel of things I did before my phd life

I received my BA from Cornell University, where I double-majored in Government and Information Science. I focused on revolutions and ideology in the former and data science + ethics in the latter — a slightly strange mix that brought me to where I am now. As an undergrad, I was fortunate to work with a variety of folks: the NYSWRI, the Get Your Green Back Tompkins campaign, and Dr. Victor Nee over at the Center for the Study of Economy and Society. Between Cornell and Northwestern, I was an Emma Bowen Foundation Summer Fellow at Reveal / Center for Investigative Reporting on the Audience Team.
