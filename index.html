+ before-phd-life

I received my BA from Cornell University, where I double-majored in Government and Information Science. I focused on revolutions and ideology in the former and data science + ethics in the latter — a kind of weird mix that brought me to where I am now. As an undergrad, I was fortunate to work with a variety of folks: the NYSWRI, the Get Your Green Back Tompkins campaign, and Dr. Victor Nee over at the Center for the Study of Economy and Society. Between Cornell and Northwestern, I was an Emma Bowen Foundation Summer Fellow at Reveal / Center for Investigative Reporting on the Audience Team.
