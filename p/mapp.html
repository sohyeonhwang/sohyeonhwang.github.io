Privacy and policies in online spaces
Call for research study interview participants!

Northwestern IRB: STU00219666
PIs: Aaron Shaw, Yan Shvartzshnaider


A collaborative research team (Northwestern University, York University) is seeking individuals to participate in interviews for a research study on privacy and policies online (Northwestern IRB #STU00219666, York ERB #3969).

Our goal is to understand how we can develop better strategies, policies, and tools to protect user rights like privacy. If you're on the Fediverse (e.g., Mastodon, Pleroma, kbin, etc.), we would love to schedule a time to talk with you!

Participants do NOT need to be familiar with any policies to participate, and no preparation is required. We expect most interviews will run between 50 and 90 minutes. Participants will receive an Amazon eGift card with a value of $20 USD.

If you are interested in participating, please fill out the screener survey and we'll be in touch soon:
[SCREENER SURVEY LINK]

In order for us to interview you, you must be an adult active on the Fediverse who is willing and able to record an interview in English with a researcher from our team. Interviews will be conducted remotely via video chat (you can keep your camera off if you prefer). We will not disclose any identifying information about you. While we may quote you in our study, we take several measures to ensure you cannot be identified through quotes or in any resulting publications.
