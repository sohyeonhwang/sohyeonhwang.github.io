Winter 2022

In the Winter quarter, I'm TAing for COMM_ST 378: Online Communities and Crowds, taught by Prof. Aaron Shaw. If you're a student in the course and/or in one of my undergraduate sections, you can find the syllabus and information about the course here. The subpage for undergraduates has the section policies. My office hours will be:

  • (remote for entire quarter) Mondays 1PM-2PM CT, virtually at: https://meet.jit.si/sohw
  • (starting 1/25) Tuesdays 11AM-1PM CT, at room 2-430 in Frances Searle (up the main stairs from the lobby, and then go right)

If you need to contact me for whatever reason* or schedule separate office hours (in-person or secure video-calling), please feel empowered to come by or simply email me!


* I realize that office hours can be intimidating and nebulous, so I just want to note that you're welcome to ask about any/various course and school-related concerns that you think I might be helpful with (which is to say, office hours definitely do not mean you are in trouble! instead, they are resources that students should take advantage of, e.g. when I was in undergrad I went to office hours with a professor to ask about the phd program they graduated from); I will do my best to help or direct you to the right places.
